Glossary

You will find definitions of words that may be difficult for you to understand.

Feminism

Doctrine that advocates equality between men and women, and the extension of the role of women in society.

Feminist movement

Movement for the improvement and extension of the role and rights of the role and rights of women in society.

Nobel Peace Prize

The Nobel Peace Prize is awarded to "the individual or community who has made the greatest or best contributed most or best to the rapprochement of peoples, to the abolition or reduction of standing armies to the gathering and propagation of progress for peace" according to the wishes, defined by by the will of Alfred Nobel.
